⚙️ First-time thermal printer setup
  1. Select your thermal printer (not PDF)
  2. Click More settings
  3. Paper size → 80mm × 200mm (or your roll size)
  4. Margins → None  |  Scale → 100%
  5. Uncheck Headers and footers
Chrome remembers these settings once set.
